Dear visitor,

Hi, I'm Daniel Jhon — but most people just call me DJ. I'm currently studying Information Technology at the University of Mindanao, though my path here wasn't exactly linear. I came from Philippine Women's College, where I took up Arts and Design under the TVL strand — so trading sketchpads for syntax was a curve, but one that taught me more than I expected: my eye for composition, color, and visual storytelling didn't disappear when I switched fields — it just found a new canvas in interfaces and layouts.

In senior high, I served as our TVL Representative and was part of the school's SGC Student Council, which sharpened my interest in leadership and working with people — something I try to carry into how I collaborate on projects today.

Right now, I'm expanding my skill set through Python, React, networking, and AI-focused coursework — particularly drawn to how AI can be woven into everyday web apps to make them feel a little more intuitive, a little more alive. Outside of code, I'm still an artist and a reader at heart — Kafka and Dostoevsky are regulars on my shelf, and when I draw, I gravitate toward surrealism with a cartoonish edge.

I'm currently on the lookout for internship/OJT opportunities where I can put both halves of my brain — the designer and the developer — to work. Got a project in mind or just want to talk shop? Reach out — the links are at the bottom of this page.

— DJ

My Services

Web Design

I shape pages the way a careful writer shapes a paragraph: with balance, rhythm, and a little breathing room, so the first glance already feels like an invitation.

Frontend Development

I build interfaces that hold together like a well-sealed envelope, responsive, readable, and steady, so the message inside reaches people without friction.

Student Projects
